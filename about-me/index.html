About me

Who am I?

My name is Girish Budhwani, based out of Bangalore, India. I did my Bachelor’s from IIT Jodhpur, currently working as a Software Developer at Microsoft.

Why SportyGeek?

Now coding and sports help me get out of bed in the morning. I have played Tennis and Cricket at college level, won a couple of medals. Currently playing Snooker and TT at office and Football on the weekends. I also love travelling and have visited six countries.
